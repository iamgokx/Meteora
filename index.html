METEORA

PARADE INTO PARADICE
1 - 2 February 2024
Meteora is the flagship event of Don Bosco College. Don Bosco College is renowned for organizing grand and vibrant events whether it might be inter-class, inter-departmental or even intercollegiate. These events create opportunities for students from within the college and across the state of Goa. The one event that takes steals the spotlight every year is, Meteora! This flagship event of Don Bosco College, conducted for 2 days, aims to bring in students across the state to showcase their talent. Meteora has been the highlight event of Don Bosco College, for the past 17 years and has excelled time after time, year after year.
General Rules
  1. Registration fee for every team is 3000/- and the last day of registration is 28th January 2024.
  2. Participating teams need to report to the registration desk at 8.30 am. compulsorily with the ID cards of all the respective team participants (Presence for the inaugural is mandatory)
  3. Each team will be allotted a circus-carnival element as their team name by drawing lots.
  4. Repeat participation for team members is allowed only if the timings of the events they want to participate in does not clash.
  5. There will be an overall champion and runners - up, determined by the cumulative score of each team at the end of every event.
  6. Participants are requested to report to the respective event venue at least 15 minutes prior to its commencement. Any delay during the performance will lead to disqualification of the team.
  7. Exceeding the time limit for any event will lead to deduction of marks
  8. Participants are requested to carry an extra pair of clothes, necessary for certain sports events.
  9. Vulgarity in any form (language/body gestures / actions /clothing /cheering slogans, etc). will lead to direct disqualification of the team.
  10. Last day to send in the registration entries is on or before 28 January 2024
  11. Any audio/video files needed for an event, must be uploaded on a link, which will be provided once the registration is confirmed.
  12. The organizing committee holds rights to alter details of the event, if necessary.
  13. For further queries, email us at: meteora24dbc@gmail.com
